Neighborhood

Cannery Row Apartment community is located in the heart of Old Town Sherwood, Oregon; Just steps away from retail shops, dining, parks and schools. Here are a few of our favorite things:

  • Pick up some dessert from Sweet Story. Their cinnamon rolls taste like they were just pulled out of moms oven!

  • Date night at Da Mimmo. What's better for a date night than cozy delicious Italian cuisine?

  • Score donut points with your office mates when you pick up a dozen (or two) donut holes from Sesame Donuts for your Wednesday meeting.

  • Looking for that one special piece to finish off your eclectic home style? From mason jars to architectural signage, there's a little bit of everything at the Railroad Street Antique Mall.

  • An outing with the kids to The Painted Plate is sure to bring home a lovely keepsake and give you quality family time.

  • Walk to your yoga or cardio kick boxing class at Escape to Yoga. Gentle Hatha with Annette is the perfect beginner class.

  • Be sure to check out the recently remodeled Sherwood Public Library.

  • Order the bourbon French toast during Sunday brunch at Fat Milo's. Only available on Sundays!, reservations suggested.

  • In the mood for tavern food? Clancy's is the place to be! They have a lovely patio for outdoor seating, minors welcome during the day, a great house drink menu and trivia for the adults!

  • Grab your morning Stumptown Americano and a stuffed croissant from Symposium Coffee. Then come back for lunch!

  • Check out the Saturday Farmers Market at Cannery Square Park, May through September.
